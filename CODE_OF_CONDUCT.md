# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want AutoFuse to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone,
regardless of experience, background, or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, pull requests, and discussions.
- Assume good intent; critique ideas, not people.
- Keep reports and feedback specific and actionable.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Trolling, deliberate disruption, or sharing others' private information.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions —
and to public spaces when representing the project.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior via [GitHub private vulnerability reporting](https://github.com/Fasen24-AI/autofuse/security/advisories/new)
or a direct message to the maintainer. Reports are reviewed confidentially,
and maintainers may remove content or block contributors who violate this code.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
